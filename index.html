How Kirkify works

Kirkify runs a custom AI model that swaps the face in your photo with Charlie and keeps the rest of the image intact.

1

Upload a clear photo

Pick an image where the face is visible and not too tiny. Front or slight angle works best.

2

AI spins up on the GPU

Our GPU server wakes up, loads the models, and runs the kirkification pipeline on your image.

3

Compare & download

Use the slider to compare original vs kirkified, then download the result in one click.

FAQ

Quick answers about Kirkify, privacy, and usage.

Is Kirkify free?
Yes. Kirkify is currently free to use and does not require an account.
Do you store my images?
Images are sent to the server only for processing and are not meant to be kept longer than needed for the kirkification step. See the Privacy Policy for details.
What kind of photos work best?
Use photos with a single face, good lighting, and a front or slightly angled view for the best kirkified result with Charlie.
Can I use the results anywhere?
You’re responsible for how you use the images. Please follow local laws and respect the rights of other people whose photos you kirkify.
